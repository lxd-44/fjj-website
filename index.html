Questions Answered

COMMON
QUESTIONS

Absolutely not. Most of our members started with zero experience. We teach you everything from scratch—how to move, fall safely, and build from there. Your first class is free, so come try it out with no pressure.

Comfortable athletic wear works great—shorts/leggings and a t-shirt. We have loaner gis (the traditional uniform) if you want to try a gi class. Just avoid anything with zippers or pockets that could catch fingers.

For beginners, 2-3 times per week is the sweet spot. Enough to build skills and see progress, but still allowing your body to recover. Once you're hooked (and you will be), many members train 4-5 times per week.

BJJ is one of the safest martial arts to train. There's no striking, and the tap system means you can stop any submission before it causes injury. We prioritise safety, and more experienced partners always look out for beginners.
