Common questions

FAQ

Do I need any experience to start?

+

Absolutely not. Most members started with zero experience. We teach you everything from scratch. Your first class is free — just show up in comfortable clothes and we'll handle the rest.

What should I wear to my first class?

+

Comfortable athletic wear — shorts or leggings and a t-shirt. We have loaner gis if you want to try. Avoid anything with zippers or pockets that could catch.

How often should I train?

+

For beginners, 2-3 times per week is the sweet spot. Enough to build skill without burning out. Once you're hooked, many members train 4-5 times per week.

Is BJJ safe? Will I get hurt?

+

BJJ is one of the safest martial arts to train. There's no striking, and the tap system means you control the intensity. Tap early, tap often — that's how everyone trains smart.

What ages do you teach?

+

Kids from age 3, teens, and adults of all ages. We have dedicated juniors classes (J1: ages 3-7, J2: ages 8-15) and adult classes for all levels.
