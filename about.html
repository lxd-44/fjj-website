Our Story

ABOUT FJJ

More than a gym. More than training. A community built on respect, growth, and the pursuit of excellence.

WHERE IT ALL BEGAN

Francis Jiu Jitsu was founded with a simple belief: everyone deserves a place to train, grow, and belong. No egos. No attitudes. Just people helping people get better.

Located in Leeming, Perth, we've built a community where first-timers train alongside competitors, where parents learn next to their kids, and where the journey matters as much as the destination.

Whether you're looking for fitness, self-defence, competition prep, or just a second family—you'll find it here.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

Our values shape everything we do—how we teach, how we train, how we treat each other.

Community First

We're a family. We celebrate each other's wins, support each other through struggles, and grow together.

Continuous Learning

There's always more to learn. We approach every session with curiosity, whether it's your first day or your thousandth.

Respect & Safety

We train hard, but we train smart. Every partner is treated with respect. Every session is safe for everyone.

Growth Mindset

Failures are lessons. Challenges are opportunities. Every setback is a setup for a comeback.

Inclusivity

Everyone belongs here. Regardless of age, gender, background, or experience—there's a place for you on our mats.

Excellence

We don't do average. We push each other to be better—better grapplers, better people, better versions of ourselves.
